Last updated: March 2026

1. Service Description. Breeze provides end-to-end encrypted peer-to-peer messaging. Messages are encrypted on your device before transmission. The relay server stores encrypted data temporarily (deleted within 7 days) and cannot read message content.

2. Eligibility. You must be at least 13 years old (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction) to use Breeze. If you are under 18, you must have parental consent.

3. Account. Your identity is a cryptographic key pair generated on your device. We do not store passwords, emails, or phone numbers. You are responsible for securing your device and any lock passwords you set.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: Breeze is designed to know as little about you as possible. All messages are end-to-end encrypted. We cannot read your messages, see your contacts, or access your files.

1. Data We Collect.

(a) Encrypted relay data: When a P2P connection is unavailable, messages are relayed through our server in encrypted form. This data is automatically deleted within 7 days. We cannot decrypt it.

(b) Presence heartbeats: Online/offline status with a 60-second TTL. No history stored.

(c) Anonymous aggregate counts: Total connected users (no identifiers).

(d) Payment data (multi-account): Processed by Stripe. We store only a user ID linked to your subscription. We never see your credit card number.

2. Data We Cannot Access. Message content, contact lists, files, voice messages, call audio/video, cryptographic keys, lock passwords, labels, notes, or any data stored in IndexedDB on your device.

3. Data Stored on Your Device. All messages, contacts, keys, and settings are stored locally in IndexedDB and localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly send it (encrypted) to another user.

4. Third-Party Services.

(a) Cloudflare: Hosting and CDN. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy.

(b) Stripe: Payment processing (additional accounts only). Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.

(c) STUN/TURN: WebRTC connection establishment. Only IP addresses are visible to the STUN server during connection setup.

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA).

(a) Access: Use /gdpr command to export all your data.

(b) Deletion: Use /wipe to delete all data, or Ctrl+Shift+Backspace for Panic Wipe (instant, irrecoverable).

(c) Portability: Use /export to download messages in JSON or CSV format.

(d) Consent withdrawal: Stop using the app. No account to "close" — your data exists only on your device.

6. Children. Breeze is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Retention. Relay data: 7 days max. Presence: 60 seconds. Payment records: as required by law. Device data: until you delete it.

8. Security. X25519/Ed25519 key exchange, AES-256-GCM encryption, Double Ratchet protocol, PBKDF2 key derivation, HSTS, CSP headers. See /security command for details.

9. Changes. We will update this policy as needed. The "Last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact. For privacy inquiries, use /help in the app or open an issue on GitHub.
